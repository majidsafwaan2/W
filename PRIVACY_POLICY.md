# Privacy Policy for Tilawa

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## Introduction

Tilawa ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Audio Recordings
- When you use the recitation feature, we temporarily record your audio to provide transcription and feedback
- Audio recordings are processed in real-time and are **not stored** on our servers
- Audio files are automatically deleted from your device after processing

### Usage Data
- We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app
- This includes features used, app crashes, and performance metrics
- No personally identifiable information is collected

### Device Information
- Device type and operating system version
- App version
- Language preferences

## How We Use Your Information

1. **Audio Transcription**: Your audio recordings are sent to our secure backend server, which uses OpenAI's Whisper API to transcribe your recitation. The audio is processed and immediately discarded.

2. **App Improvement**: We use anonymous analytics to understand how users interact with the app and improve the user experience.

3. **Error Tracking**: We use error tracking services to identify and fix bugs in the app.

## Third-Party Services

### OpenAI Whisper API
- We use OpenAI's Whisper API for audio transcription
- Audio is sent securely to OpenAI's servers for processing
- OpenAI's privacy policy applies: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

### Analytics Services
- **Firebase Analytics**: Anonymous usage analytics (Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Sentry**: Error tracking and crash reporting (Sentry Privacy Policy: https://sentry.io/privacy/)

## Data Storage

- Audio recordings are **not stored** on our servers
- Audio files are temporarily stored on your device and automatically deleted after processing
- App settings and bookmarks are stored locally on your device

## Data Security

- All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
- API keys and sensitive information are stored securely on our backend servers
- We implement rate limiting to prevent abuse

## Your Rights

- You can stop using the app at any time
- Audio recordings are automatically deleted after processing
- You can clear app data through your device settings

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app.
